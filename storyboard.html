PAGES AND THEIR PURPOSE

home/index page

The 'Home/Index Page' is designed to introduce individuals to Amanda Johnson, and the factors and attributes that makes her up as a person. The introductory page serves as a means to allow persons to get to know Amanda on a deeper level. An introductory audio message, hobbies, likes, dislikes, and favourite movie scence are highlighted on this webpage.

               

professional page

The "Professional Page" is designed with the aim of presenting a resume for my future self. Using a modern design template, it displays the places I would like to work, the work I will get to do, professional vision and mission statemens, and illustrates the job requirements and qualifications of the career I would like to have when I leave University.

               

personality page

The page titled "Personality" serves to provide an insightful exploration of my personal traits, hobbies, and their impact on my academic life. It aims to shed light on how my strongest trait influence my approach to academic pursuit. Additionally, it delves into a quote from a hero figure in my life, explaining the reasons behind my admiration for this individual. Furthermore, the page includes an assessment of my personality through the online tool, Keirsey Temperament Sorter. This analysis involves a screenshot of the results and reflecting on the validity of the test both generally and in my unique case, offering a well-rounded perspective on the assessment.

               

personal development page

The "Personal Development Page" is designed with the intent of fostering growth, self-reflection. It aims to provide meaningful engagement with the book "Twelve Pillars" by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener, enabling a deeper understanding of the principles discussed and how I could apply them in my life. Additionally, this page highlights the name of the degree I am working towards, and the top three skills I have acquired thus far.

               

storyboard page

The "Storyboard Page" is created with the intent of providing a conceptual framework for my website. It aims to visualize and organize the design and structure of the website, ensuring a cohesive and purposeful user experience. The page encompasses various essential elements that contribute to the development of the website. These include: a wireframe, which involves presenting a digital layout design for the pages. Secondly the pages and their purposes are outlined. Thirdly, the theme is defined, which includes the colour, graphics, and target audience of the website. Additionally, a comprehensive list of features to be incorporated into the website is presented. Lastly, the page concludes by providing a hosting link, offering a direct pathway to access the live or hosted version of the website.

               

reference page

The "Reference Page" is crafted with the clear intent of providing a comprehensive and organized list of bibliographic sources used to gather information for the website. It adheres to the APA 7th edition format, ensuring proper citation of the sources consulted.



WEBSITE THEME

  • Colour:

    • Purple, blue, orange and pink where chosen as the color palette for The Amanda Chronicles. These colors create a visually appealing and balanced palette that reflects the diverse and creative nature of the website. They evoke a range of emotions, from calmness and trust to excitement and creativity, enhancing the overall user experience and encouraging engagement with the content. The chosen colors resonate with the themes and objectives of "The Amanda Chronicles" and contribute to the website's unique identity.


  • Graphics:

    • Graphics were strategically utilized on "The Amanda Chronicles" website to enhance user engagement, convey the website's theme, and provide visual appeal.


  • Target Audience:

    • The Amanda Chronicles is targeted towards the general public, and is not limited to persons belonging to a specific background. My diverse interests, including anime, art, reading, and watching shows and movies, have broad appeal across different age groups and backgrounds. Thus the website content caters to these varied interests, attracting a wide range of individuals.
